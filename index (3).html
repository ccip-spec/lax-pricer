NOTES & LIMITATIONS

Strong markets: ML, Spread, Total, Player Goals/Assists/Points/Saves/GBs. Bivariate Poisson on a tuned team strength is the right tool.

v2 upgrades: First Goalscorer now blends empirical hit rate (from ~70 PBP-extracted first goals) with share model. Player props use Negative Binomial when game-log variance shows overdispersion (typical for high-volume scorers).

Still approximated: Faceoff Wins (uses primary FO guy, applies team FO%). Empirical first-goal sample is small (3-10 games per team), so margin remains juiced.

Low-confidence markets: Anytime/2+/3+ goalscorer for low-volume players. Use higher margins.

Sample size: 12-17 games per team. Strength regression is on by default (25% pull toward league mean).

Final Four / Championship: Treated as neutral per user spec, even though hosted at Charlottesville.
